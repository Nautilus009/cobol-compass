# Hotfix Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist before proposing or approving an urgent COBOL production fix.

## Review

- Defect is clearly understood.
- Affected dialect, platform, and runtimes are identified.
- Change is minimal and scoped.
- Business behavior outside the defect remains unchanged.
- Compile command and target environment are known.
- Focused test plan exists.
- Rollback or recovery plan exists.
- Operational logs and return codes remain compatible.
- Human review is complete.
- Change-control evidence is captured.
